Peanut Butter Cups

Description

I wish that I could eat 10,000 of these in one sitting. 4 simple ingredients, infinite amount of joy & flavor.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (129 g) creamy peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • ¼ cup (31 g) confectioners' sugar
  • 2 cups (364g) milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Line a standard 12-cup muffin tin with liners. Set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl add peanut butter, butter, and confectioners' sugar. Use a hand mixer on low and combine all of the ingredients. Set aside.
  3. Add the chocolate to a microwave-safe bowl. Heat in 20-second increments, stirring after every 20 seconds, until the chocolate is melted and creamy (about 1-2 minutes total).
  4. Add about 1 tablespoon of chocolate to the bottom of each muffin tin (only enough to fully coat the bottom of the liner). Top with 2 heaping teaspoons of the peanut butter mixture in the center. Add about 1 tablespoon of chocolate to the top of each cup. Carefully tap the muffin tin onto the counter to help the chocolate settle and smooth out the tops.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour, or until set, before serving.
